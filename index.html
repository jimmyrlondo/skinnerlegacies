Who we are

A supportive partner for meaningful behavioral health outcomes.

Skinner Legacies is building a beautiful, welcoming experience around services that make a real difference in daily life. We focus on coordinated support, restorative care, crisis response, and individualized planning that helps clients and families feel seen, supported, and empowered.

Core service pillars

Programs built to support the full picture of care.

These overviews are editable placeholders that can be refined later as services, eligibility, and program details are finalized.

Pillar 01

General Services

Flexible support options designed to help individuals and families access the care, resources, and guidance they need to move forward with confidence.

Pillar 02

Community Psychiatric Support Treatment

Ongoing community-based support that helps individuals strengthen coping skills, improve stability, and connect behavioral health goals to everyday routines.

Pillar 03

Psychosocial Rehabilitative Services

Skill-building services that promote independence, confidence, and healthier social functioning at home, in the community, and in daily life.

Pillar 04

Therapeutic Behavioral Health Services

Therapeutic support focused on emotional wellness, behavioral growth, and practical strategies that help clients navigate challenges with greater resilience.

Pillar 05

Targeted Case Management

Coordinated planning and advocacy to help clients access providers, benefits, referrals, and supports that align with their specific goals and circumstances.

Pillar 06

Mental Health Day Treatment

Structured daytime care that supports routine, therapeutic engagement, social interaction, and continued progress in a safe and supportive setting.

Pillar 07

OhioRise Behavioral Respite

Restorative respite services that give caregivers relief while ensuring youth and families continue receiving attentive, compassionate, and stabilizing support.

Pillar 08

Crisis Intervention

Responsive support during critical moments to help de-escalate challenges, strengthen safety, and connect individuals and families with the next right steps.
