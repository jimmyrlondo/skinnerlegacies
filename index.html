About Skinner Legacies

A trusted space for care, connection, and forward movement.

Skinner Legacies is designed to support individuals and families through services that strengthen emotional wellness, build daily stability, and connect people with the care they need. This site can continue to evolve as programs, team information, and service details grow.

General Services

Flexible support options that help individuals and families access care, guidance, and resources that fit their needs.

Community Psychiatric Support Treatment

Community-based support focused on stability, coping skills, daily functioning, and progress toward behavioral health goals.

Psychosocial Rehabilitative Services

Skill-building support that encourages independence, confidence, and stronger social and emotional functioning.

Therapeutic Behavioral Health Services

Therapeutic care that supports emotional wellness, behavioral growth, and practical next steps for individuals and families.

Targeted Case Management

Coordinated planning and advocacy that helps connect clients to providers, services, referrals, and supportive resources.

Mental Health Day Treatment

Structured daytime support that encourages routine, engagement, therapeutic participation, and ongoing progress.

OhioRise Behavioral Respite

Respite support that offers relief for caregivers while maintaining attentive, stabilizing care for youth and families.

Crisis Intervention

Responsive care during urgent moments to help de-escalate challenges, support safety, and guide families toward next steps.
